About KPR furniture

Trained at RIT's School for American Crafts by many of the founders of the studio furniture movement, I am most interested in sculptural forms as functional furniture. My work embraces the utility of both hand tools and computer-aided machines and I work in an expedient mixture of easily available, traditional and modern materials. I work primarily at my studio on Railroad Street in Rochester, NY, half a block from the Rochester Public Market, where I can get great coffee and empanadas at lunch several days a week.
